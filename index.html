What is this?Restart

In this game you need to try to reverse a list of numbers. You do this by splitting an joining the numbers subject to some constraints:

  • You can't create a number larger than the largest number in the initial list
  • You can never have the same number in the list twice
    • You can move numbers around by splitting and merging.

    Enjoy!
